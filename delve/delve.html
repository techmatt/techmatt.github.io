Welcome to the Delve

The Delve is a computer-aided choose-your-own adventure. You and your chosen companions will explore the underground complex of Siru-Kisal. You may explore with as few as one person, or with the entirety of game.

Once you enter the Delve from one of the entrances listed to the right, you will have 15 minutes during which you may safely explore.

In the Delve you may find documents or treasures, remanants of past. When you encounter a room with items, click on them to take possession and take the item from the indicated location in gamespace.

The only consequences for failing in the Delve is immediate ejection. As a player you should explore without fear, although your character may interpret these as narrow and frightening escapes.

In these twisting passages you will not enounter other groups and if you choose to separate you will not be able to reconvene until you escape the Delve.

Click on an entrance to begin. Most buttons require pressing-and-holding and your choices cannot be reverted.

Buttons on the header row of the Delve may be activated by any character in the group. Actions/connections below the room text may only be taken by group consensus.

How to Split the Party

Your delving group may split up at any time, but if you do so, you will not meet up until after the delve.

To do so, any number of people may take note of the "Split Code" below, relocate to another delve client, and input the code to resume thier delve.

Note that you may not contribute an aspect or anything else towards a connection requirement unless you are taking that connection with the group. (You may not contribute the group travelling down a connection while you stay behind.)

If either of the Phi or Delta panels are revealed, press and hold on them to unreveal them if the person who activated those abilities are no longer in your party.
